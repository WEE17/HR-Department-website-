Leave Request Leave Request Policies On Leave-Vacation















    Assisted Conception (IVF) Leave



  1. The Trust will support employees with time off for the purpose of undergoing programmes of treatment.



  2. Employees with 12 months’ continuous service with the Trust will be entitled to a maximum of 5 days’
    (pro-rata for part-time staff) paid time off in any 12 month period, to attend appointments and programmes of treatment.
    There is no requirement for the time off to be taken in full-day blocks.


  3. Authorised unpaid leave will be granted to employees with less than 12
    months’ continuous service with the Trust, or those who have used
    their full paid leave allowance. Alternatives may include annual leave, or the use of flexitime.

  4. Employees must inform their line manager of their intention to take the
    leave, giving reasonable notice of the leave. Line managers reserve the
    right to request documentation in support of the request for leave.

    1. Date of issue

    2. Date of procedure

    3. A statement as to whether or not the employer is unable to perform based on the nature of the procedure.
